Column 1 is the Subject (ranging from 1 - 30)

Column 2 is the Activity Label that is being performed by the subject.

Column 3: 81 are all averaged values per subject per activity based on the mean and standard deviation data available as described below using the original README file from the data set.

For each record it is provided:
======================================
- Identifiers of the subject who carried out the experiment and the activity being carried out.
- A 79-feature vector with averaged time and frequency domain mean and std variables. 
- Activity labels with the name remaining intact as the original dataset.
